Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 April 2025

1. What we collect

  • Account data — username and a hashed (non-reversible) version of your password. We do not collect your email address.
  • Usage data — questions you add, completions, ratings, challenges, and credits. This is needed to operate the platform.
  • Images — any images you upload with a question are stored on our server and displayed publicly to other users.
  • Session data — a signed cookie is stored in your browser to keep you logged in. It contains no personal information beyond your user ID.

2. How we use it

All data collected is used solely to operate QBank — to display questions, track credits, and personalise your study experience. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Who can see your data

  • Your username and the questions you post are visible to all logged-in users.
  • Your completion history, ratings, and credit balance are visible only to you (via your profile).
  • Uploaded images are publicly accessible via their URL.

4. Third parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. QBank uses:

  • jsDelivr CDN — to load KaTeX for equation rendering (your browser fetches a JS file; no personal data is sent). These files are cached locally after the first visit.
  • Desmos — if you embed a Desmos graph link, your browser loads that graph from Desmos' servers directly.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you would like your account or content removed, contact us via the GitHub repository.

6. Security

Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — we cannot see your password. All other data is stored in a database accessible only to the platform operator. No system is perfectly secure; use a unique password for QBank.

7. Children's privacy

QBank is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of QBank after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? Reach us via the GitHub repository linked in the site footer.
